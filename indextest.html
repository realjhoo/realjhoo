About Me

I wrote my first program in BASIC on a Sinclair, a small computer made entirely of stone knived and bearskins. From there, I learned VisualBasic, Pascal, Delphi, and a smattering of other languages. In college, I made money on the side coding personal websites for local Boomers who didnt know why they needed a website, but wanted one anyway.

After college, I went on to work in various musums in the Houston area. There, I used my limited skills to maintain and update their aging websites.
